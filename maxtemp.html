Max Temperature

TAs we get closer to the equator line, where the latitude is zero, the temperature rises, and it decreases as we move away from it, confirming our expectation. However, it is very interesting to compare cities with the same latitude in opposite hemispheres (Northern vs. Southern), since we oberve there is a slight concnetration of the distribution of cities in higher temperatures in the Northern Hemisphere.
