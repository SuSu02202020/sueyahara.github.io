Cloudiness

There is not a observable trend that correlates latitude and cloudiness. However, it is very interesting to note there are bands of cities at 0, 20 and 80 cloudiness. It would be interesting to compare historical data from different times of the day distributed along time of the year (different seasons) to establish if there is correlation between time of the day, time of the year (season), latitude and cloudiness.
